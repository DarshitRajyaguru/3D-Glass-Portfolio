My Journey

The Story So Far

It started with a curiosity — how does the web actually work? That question led me down a path that would consume years of late nights, countless experiments, and an ever-growing love for building things that actually help people.

My first real WordPress project was a simple business site. But as I dug deeper — into PHP hooks, custom post types, the block editor, REST API — I realized WordPress wasn't just a CMS. It was a platform.

Contributing to WordPress Core felt like a natural next step. Getting my first release credit was a milestone that reminded me: the tools we use every day are built by people just like us. I became one of those people — 12 times over.

Today, I blend classic WordPress craftsmanship with AI-powered workflows to deliver results faster, smarter, and with more impact than ever before. The next chapter? Even bigger.
